About Me

With 4 years of academic grounding in Agricultural Engineering (Bachelor’s with Honors) and more than 2 years of hands-on experience in Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), and Internet of Things (IoT), I’m deeply engaged in bridging the gap between agriculture and cutting-edge technology.

Currently, I’m delivering training sessions to university students at the University of Rwanda – Musanze Busogo campus, under the INESAM Academy. I focus on equipping students with practical and industry-relevant knowledge in AI, ML, and IoT, especially as applied in the agricultural sector. Seeing how these emerging technologies can empower young minds to think innovatively and solve real-world problems is one of the most rewarding parts of my journey.

Beyond teaching, I’ve been actively involved in developing and implementing AI and IoT solutions tailored to agricultural needs ranging from precision farming to smart irrigation and real-time crop monitoring. These experiences have sharpened my skills in project planning, team collaboration, and tech deployment in real-world agricultural environments.

With a strong foundation in agricultural engineering and a deep passion for tech-driven innovation, I’m well-positioned to lead and contribute to projects that aim to transform agriculture and related fields. Whether it’s through research, development, or capacity building, I believe in using technology as a tool for positive impact especially in communities where innovation can be a game-changer.

I’m always open to new collaborations, knowledge-sharing, and opportunities to contribute to meaningful projects. If you're working at the intersection of agriculture and technology, or if you're passionate about digital transformation and sustainability, let’s connect and build something impactful together!

Projects

Received the Global People Award UK (United Kingdom) under the Efficiency for Access design challenge, supported by the Energy Saving Trust and Engineers Without Borders UK, as part of the Project Research Competition 2024–2025. Following this success, I co-founded and now serve as the Chief Executive Officer (CEO) of INESAM Company Ltd, established after winning the International Multi Disciplinary Competition 2024–2025. Fortunately, 15 trained in INESAM Academy won grant from MasterCard.
